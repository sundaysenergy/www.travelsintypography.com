If type is your trip ...

… you should join us for an excursion through some of the crowned heads of European Typography. This site documents the finest examples of signs, studios and printers working in Spain, Germany and Italy. We will be embarking again on May 17 till June 7, 2014 and over the following three weeks documenting historic letterforms and living legends who are keeping letterpress alive.

To see what we’ve been up to simply click on the map link above and check out the samples of letterforms we’ve been collecting. Look for updates soon.
