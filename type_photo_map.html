Type Photos

Each marker indicates a type-related image found in one of our visited cities.
Visitors are invited to add their own photos of typography to the map.
Use the zoom tools provided in the upper left hand corner to navigate the map below and find where each piece of typography has come from. Clicking on a marker will bring up a viewable image of the type as well as provide information on exact GPS coordinates, when it was taken and by whom.
